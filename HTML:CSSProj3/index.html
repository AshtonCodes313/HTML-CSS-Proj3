What Naruto is About

Naruto is about a 12 - 16 year old kid that lost his parents when he was born.
He lives in the village hidden in the leafs and wishes to become hokage (the lead ninja).
Everybody looks down on him and calls him a freak because he has a demon fox inside him.
He struggled trying to become a ninja and was always made fun of.
Naruto was put on a team with his rival, Sasuke.
Sasuke left the village in hope of getting power.
Naruto dedicated his life to bringing Sasuke back to the village.

Cool things about Naruto

  1. Naruto loves to eat Ramen.
  2. Naruto gets his power from a demon inside him named Kurama.
  3. His weakness is Ramen.

Sad things in Naruto's past

  • Naruto's parents died saving him.
  • Everybody thought of Naruto as an outcast.
  • Naruto had no friends.
